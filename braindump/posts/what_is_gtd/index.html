It is mostly a set of best practices to make room in your head.

We have times we are more clever and inspired and times we are not. gtd helps using the former to give the latter instructions.

It also is more about being appropriately engaged rather than doing things. It is about being 100% focused on the task at hand, because you know you can trust a system to store the engagements so that you can be on cruise control.
