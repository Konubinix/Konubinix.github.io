key factor when placing your outdoor temperature sensor is installing it at eye level. Installing it too low or too high might give you inaccurate readings since air temperatures are cooler near ground level and warmer near upper levels because of convection currents in the atmosphere. So if possible, install your sensor around 4-5 feet off the ground for maximum accuracy.
