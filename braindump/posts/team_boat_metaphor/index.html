Team/Boat Metaphor

Fleeting

  • people paddle to make the team go forward,
  • a limited amount of people are at the helm,
  • the boat has a direction, the common goal of the team,
  • everyone needs to make the effort in the direction of the goal or the journey will be harder,
    • hence the need for
      • all the team members to be sure they understand the goal, or they might paddle backwards,
      • people at the helm to make it understood,
      • all the team to overcome the false consensus effect,
  • the journey might not be easy and impediments may arise,
