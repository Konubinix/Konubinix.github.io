aristotle on happiness as the ultimate good

  • External reference:

It seems that the only ultimate goal would be eudaimonia, translated as something like “flourishing”.

Yet, depending on who speaks, eudaimonia might be pretty different. Aristotle included wealth and health in its definition, while stoicism only define it with virtue ethics.
