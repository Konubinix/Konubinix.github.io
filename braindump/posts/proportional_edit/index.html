Proportional Edit is a way of transforming selected elements (such as vertices) while having that transformation affect other nearby elements

— https://docs.blender.org/manual/en/latest/editors/3dview/object/editing/transform/control/proportional_edit.html

You can increase or decrease the radius of the proportional editing influence with the mouse wheel WheelUp, WheelDown or PageUp, PageDown respectively

— https://docs.blender.org/manual/en/latest/editors/3dview/object/editing/transform/control/proportional_edit.html
