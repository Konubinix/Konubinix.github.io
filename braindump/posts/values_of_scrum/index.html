values of scrum

— https://www.scrum.org

commitment (scrum)

see engagement.

Needed to allow inspection and adaptation.

The scrum team is committed to deliver the unique sprint goal.

focus (scrum)

Needed to allow inspection and adaptation.

The team focuses on the sprint goal it has defined to and the product goal the product owner has defined. The scrum events allow inspection at regular intervals so as to adaptation.

focus is on the work of the Sprint to make the best possible progress toward these goals

— https://scrumguides.org/scrum-guide.html

openness (scrum)

Needed to allow adaptation and transparency. Without it, the team might be tempted to hide issues and thus avoid dealing with them.

respect (scrum)

Needed to allow adaptation and transparency. Without it, the team might be tempted to hide issues and thus avoid dealing with them.

courage (scrum)

Needed to allow adaptation. Without it, the team might be tempted to avoid dealing with issue even though it has realized them by inspection.

The Scrum Team members have the courage to do the right thing, to work on tough problems.

— https://scrumguides.org/scrum-guide.html
