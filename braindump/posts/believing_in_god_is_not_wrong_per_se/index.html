Believing in God Is Not Wrong per Se

Fleeting

People believe in some god, people believe of « Science », people believe in whatever they like. After all, tous les modèles sont faux, certains sont utiles. If at some point believing in god gives useful predictions, it would be sad not to use it just on principle.

I think the debate with beliefs is when it impacts moral intuitions. But it does not depend on the belief system. People that would have scientific explanations for moral intuitions would be wrong (at least, it is what I decided to believe) just alike.
