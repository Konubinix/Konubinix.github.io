gtd and scrum

  • a trusted system in both,
  • same values, like courage and transparence,
  • both need epistemic modesty
  • inspection (scrum),
  • continuous improvement,
  • some rules to move things forward
  • the fall of the wagon hypothesis
  • short iterations,
  • what done means == definition of done,
  • appropriately engaged == product owner,
  • weekly review == sprint,
  • gtd in 5 steps == grooming + planning + sprint + retrospective,
  • rapid refocusing == sprint -> only focus on few things but be 100% focused on it, in contrast with remembering stuffs you won’t move in the near future.
  • both try to say the bare minimum and no more -> lean
