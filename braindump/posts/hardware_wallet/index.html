A hardware wallet is a special type of bitcoin wallet which stores the user’s private keys in a secure hardware device.

— https://docs.iota.org/docs/wallets/0.1/trinity/concepts/hardware-wallet

A hardware wallet, sometimes referred to as cold storage, is a physical electronic device that stores your seed and signs bundles for you offline.
