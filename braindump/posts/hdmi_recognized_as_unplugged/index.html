HDMI Recognized as "Unplugged"

Fleeting

  • Référence externe : https://askubuntu.com/questions/781726/hdmi-recognized-as-unplugged-after-installing-drivers

I have the same issue. Each time I suspend the system and wake it up, pulseaduio says HDMI is unplugged. I do the following:

Press Ctrl+Alt+F1 to go to a terminal log in.

Then press Ctrl+Alt+F7 to go back to the GUI.

After that, pulseaudio says HDMI is plugged in (under Output Device).
