Cruise Control

Fleeting

In the gtd method, this metaphor indicates that you have built a trusted system and you can rely on it, letting your brain relax (cognitive ease). Because you know your trusted system won’t let you down.

The weekly review helps making sure the system keeps being trusted, because you made sure of it at this point.
