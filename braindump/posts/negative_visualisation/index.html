Negative Visualisation

Fleeting

Have flickering thoughts of stuffs that could go wrong to realize that things are not that bad and feel gratitude. A flickering thought of your son dying may help you appreciate better the time you have with him. It should be only flickering, just enough to feel the gratitude of its contrary.

Its particularly counter intuitive and hard to do, see 15 extra IQ points.
