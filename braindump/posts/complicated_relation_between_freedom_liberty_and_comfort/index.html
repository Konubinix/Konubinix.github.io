Complicated Relation Between Freedom/Liberty and Comfort

Fleeting

Sometimes, more freedom gives more stress and more confusions.

Sometimes, less freedom also gives more stress and more confusions.

Otfen, role playing games are presented are the way to have the more freedom in gaming, because your imagination is the boundary. Yet, you still need to create some limits to have fun, like assuming that your character is human-like, or that gravity applies, or even that there exists a language between characters.

It is as if we should find the appropriate compromise between freedom and boundaries. It is even not clear whether that compromise is the same for everyone.
