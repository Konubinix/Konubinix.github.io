How to Make Edna Point to the Last Day of Next Month

Fleeting

edna provide a very powerful way of specifying scheduled and deadline. But sometimes stuff might be tricky

To point to the last day of the next month, you can:

  1. easily point to the first day of the last month with org-read-date format simply writing 1
  2. easily move one day backward in time to find the last day of current month
  3. then easily move one month forward in time to find the last day of next month
deadline!("1") deadline!("-1d") deadline!("+1m")
