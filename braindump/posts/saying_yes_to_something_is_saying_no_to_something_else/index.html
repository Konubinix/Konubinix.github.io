Saying Yes to Something Is Saying No to Something Else

Fleeting

Tools like the gtd helps feeling confident not doing what you are not doing, because you can rely on the trusted system to see what you are saying no to.

This help visualizing all the possible alternative decision you could take and decide that this one is the best you can do, even if it is not the best you would have imagined.

Also, dichotomy of control helps focusing on the subset of alternative presents that are actually possible. Along with the view from above and negative visualisation, it helps realizing that the only thing that changes is the way you feel. Therefore, you can ask yourselves whether you’d rather this situation with you being sad or the exact same situation with you being detached (stoicism).
