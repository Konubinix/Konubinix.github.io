Team Player

Fleeting

  • voir,
    • équipe,

Someone that works with the team to reach the common goal.

But not only that, it is someone that accept the decisions of the team, even when per disagree with those.

Otherwise, this is not a team player, but a single player that uses a team to help per.

Team players don’t help each other. Instead, they move together toward the common goal.
