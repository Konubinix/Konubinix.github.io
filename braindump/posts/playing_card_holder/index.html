tag: learning 3D printing

Printed on my labists x1 mini scaling on X and Z by 0.85 and rotating so that it uses the diagonal. I printed it with a 6mm brim and a 20% infill.

I could even use the extra space to put the stand and print the whole thing at once.

I was quite impressed to see how the printer was able to create a bridge between the two feet, without support.

The result was just great.

Making the stand bigger

The stand is too small and with my kids playing, the card holder often falls. So I adjusted the stand to make it larger and added a tail.

()

Reducing the brim

Using a 4mm brim, I could scale to X*0.88, Y, Z*0.88.
