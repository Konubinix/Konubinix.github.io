When doing dichotomy of control, we soon realize that only our thoughts and emotion are under our control.

You will realize that there are automatic emotions that we don’t control when stuff happen. But after some time you might realize that those emotions stick and can be controlled. You should be intellectually honest then and decide whether you want to feel them or not.
