Using Braindump and Blog

From now on, I will try to put random unstructured thoughts on the braindump and “tell stories” in the blog.

I discovered org-roam a few month ago and it changed my life. I am discovering a new world of possibilities about taking notes. I particularly love the idea of braindump, where I can just throw random ideas and link them together. I guess I will be a better way for me to share things with friends.

For instance, instead of sending a mail to a friend to share interesting french youtube channels or podcasts (podcasts que j’aime), I can now simply write the idea in my braindump and share the dump with the friend.

I don’t think I will really find the time to write anything relevant, but at least I have the perfect setup to not do it!
