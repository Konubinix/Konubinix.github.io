Printing a Chess Board and Set

tag: learning 3D printing

I would like to print those https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:40605

I have old black and a white filament spools to finish.

Yet, the white one may be too old and wet. Fortunately I still had some white-ish filament at hand.

I printed those with slic3r, applying a scale of 0.8.

Put together, they don’t look that bad, thanks to the cheerleader effect.

Somehow, the pointy ones where totally messed up. Also, it did not manage to do the king’s cross, most likely because of a lack of support.

I might try to print again some of those.

Those might also be other chess set that might be easier to print, if need be:
